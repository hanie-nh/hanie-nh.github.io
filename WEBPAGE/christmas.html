About Christmas

Christmas is a holiday celebrated on December 25th, commemorating the birth of Jesus Christ. It is a time of joy, family gatherings, and festive celebrations. The holiday has evolved over centuries, incorporating various cultural traditions and practices.

Christmas Traditions

Decorations

One of the most beloved traditions is decorating the Christmas tree. Families often gather to hang ornaments, string lights, and place a star or angel on top.

Gift-Giving

Exchanging gifts symbolizes love and generosity. Many people enjoy the excitement of finding the perfect present for their loved ones.

Festive Foods

Christmas meals vary by culture but often include roasted meats, seasonal vegetables, and delicious desserts like gingerbread cookies and fruitcake.

Christmas Recipes

Classic Christmas Cookies

Try our delicious Christmas cookie recipe! These cookies are perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients:

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup colored sugar for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C).
  2. In a small bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and baking powder.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla.
  5. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients.
  6. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls, and place onto ungreased cookie sheets. Flatten each ball with the bottom of a glass, and sprinkle with colored sugar.
  7. Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden.
